Epi.Curio infuses the flavours and scents of the past into collections of objects

It's time to not only pick up the objects behind the glass, but also to fully immerse oneself into the realities they represent. This includes tastes and smells, powerfully connected to memory, identity and perspective but rarely part of heritage experiences. It's time to bake your collection, and eat it too!
